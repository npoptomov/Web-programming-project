How to place an order?

1. Open the website

2. Add to cart the products which you want to buy.

3. Click on cart menu item

4. Click on "Proceed to checkout"

5. Fill out your personal info for ordering

6. Select a payment method and enter payment details

7. Click on "Place order" button

8. Congratulations! Your order was successfully made.
